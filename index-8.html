The Story

Born from a Passion for the Extraordinary

King Laverians was built on a simple belief: everyone deserves products that feel truly exceptional. What started as a personal quest to find items combining quality, design, and value — grew into a curated marketplace unlike any other in Kenya.

We scour the world — from artisan workshops to modern studios — to bring you a diverse selection spanning every corner of life. Fashion, home, lifestyle, rare collectibles. We say yes to everything that meets our royal standard.

Every product is personally vetted. Every customer is treated like royalty. This isn't just a store. It's a kingdom built for you.

The Royal FAQ

At checkout, enter your Safaricom M-Pesa number. You'll receive an STK Push prompt on your phone. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to confirm — payment is processed instantly and securely. No card needed.

Nairobi delivery is 24 hours. Other Kenya counties take 2–4 business days. King members enjoy free express shipping, and Emperor members in Nairobi get same-day delivery.

We offer 30-day hassle-free returns on all orders. Simply reach us on WhatsApp at 0793316778 and we'll arrange collection. Refunds are processed via M-Pesa within 24 hours.
